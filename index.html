Twitter Contest Bot

Twitter is filled tweets by companies and individuals such as "like and follow to win" and "retweet to win." contestor is a Twitter bot that finds these tweets, parses them, and then performs the action needed to enter the contest.

I ran contestor for about a month and was able to enter over 20,000 contents, and won 16. You can view what I won here.

Technologies used: Python

Earthquake Globe

Globe that visualizes recent earthquake data. Ability to view earthquakes within the past 24 hours, 7 days, or 30 days. Data is updated every 24 hours with a Python script. Built upon the Google Chrome team's WebGL Globe experiment. You can check it out by clicking here.

Technologies used: Python, JavaScript, WebGL Globe
